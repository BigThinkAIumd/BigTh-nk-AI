Join Us

Pyoneers

In pyoneers, we will take up different subtopics of AI, leads will go over the topic to explain and finally we will work on a project. Pyoneers is handled by three separate leads who go over AI, Data Science, and Python.

AI

We teach you how to use supervised machine learning models and measure performance metrics from them. Some machine learning models we’ve covered in the past include decision trees/random forests, neural networks, logistic regression, etc.

Data Science

We teach you about the pipeline of cleaning raw data, performing exploratory data analysis and feature engineering so the data is ready for the model.

Python

We teach you the basics of python programming from scratch! Some fundamental topics include loops, functions, lists, conditionals, etc

EDGE

A recent addition to the club. In edge, we will be taking up cutting ‘EDGE’ topics, research, and problems in AI and work with them.

Join Now

You can get involved by joining the club via TerpLink. If you are proficient at any one or more of the areas covered in the club, then you can also consider becoming one of the leads! Joining the club as a member or as a lead is a great opportunity to not only meet more people who specialize in the same field, but also improve your own skills and learn from your peers.
